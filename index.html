Order Sushi in New York

The "Sushi and Noodles" restaurant offers its customers the most delicious sushi with home delivery, prepared according to classic recipes adapted to the American audience, as well as our chefs' own developments. We value our customers' time, so you can order sushi in New York with home or office delivery.

Our menu includes over 20 types of sushi:

  • Classic sushi with raw salmon, tuna, and perch.
  • Exotic sushi with tiger prawns and scallops.
  • Spicy sushi with smoked salmon and eel.

The menu also includes gunkans with red caviar and tobiko filling, as well as fellyxes where Japanese mayonnaise is combined with fish, seafood, and eel. Lovers of spicy dishes can buy sushi with spicy sauce. Popular fillings include smoked chicken, snow crab, shrimp, scallops, tuna, salmon, and perch.

Experience the artistry of sushi-making combined with the convenience of home or office delivery by placing an order with "Sushi and Noodles" today. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction ensures that each bite of our delectable sushi will transport you to a culinary journey filled with authentic flavors and delightful surprises.
